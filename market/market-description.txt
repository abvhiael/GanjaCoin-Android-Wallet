Have your Ganjacoins with you always, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Ganjacoin Wallet is the first mobile Ganjacoin app for Android, and will be updated with features as they become available.
<b>FEATURES</b>

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet, hosting its own node, is purely de-centralized and completely peer to peer.
• Display of Ganjacoin amounts in 420G, m420G and µ420G.
• Conversion to and from supported national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Ganjacoin via NFC, QR-codes or Ganjacoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Ganjacoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
• App widget for Ganjacoin balance.
